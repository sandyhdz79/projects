Advent Christmas

Is a christian celebration celebrated throughout December to the upcoming days of Christmas.

Light a Candle

For the 2024 Advent Christmas year, we are light candles every Sunday and saying a prayer.

  1. Advent week 1 – Hope: Prophecy Candle (purple)
  2. Advent week 2 – Peace: Bethlehem Candle (purple)
  3. Advent week 3 – Joy: Shepherd’s Candle (pink)
  4. Advent week 4 – Love: Angel’s Candle (purple)
