A Tribute to "Payable on Death" Band

Traa, Wuv, Sonny, Marcos
"Payable on Death" known as P.O.D.

A Tribute to my favorite band, Payable on Death, known as P.O.D.

They've been making music since 1992 and are from the "619" San Diego, California.

Literally the best band ever! The genres from soft rock, reggae rock, ska punk, rap rock, rap metal, nu metal and above all they are a Christian Band. Here are the guys from the band:

  • Sonny Sandoval, throat of P.O.D.
  • Wuv Bernardo, drums
  • Marcos Curiel, lead guitar
  • Traa Daniels, bassist

My favorite 3 albums from P.O.D.:

Satellite:
Satellite was released on 09/11/2001. Tremendously grateful for this album, the music helped me and so many people get through really difficult times in America. The album has my #1 lifesong "Alive."
Testify:
Testify was released on 01/24/2006. Another great album, that was released while I was going through a really bad divorce. I love the reggae rock and the collaboration with "Matisyahu," and before she became super famous, "Katy Perry." The album describes the band on how they can testify through the music; the strength of having faith and moving forward.
Payable on Death:
Payable on Death was realsed on 11/04/2003. The album is self-titled, and speaks for itself, Payable on Death. I love this album, a little bit more heavy than the previous albums, and they had a song for the movie "The Matrix" named "Sleeping Awake"pretty cool!
